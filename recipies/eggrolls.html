Filipino Eggrolls

Description

Lumpia is a type of spring roll found in the Philippines. Often served in parties, lumpia has been a staple of a party necessity in a Filipino get together.

Ingredients

  • Shreeded carrots
  • 3 pounds ground meat - your preference
  • 1 egg
  • Diced onions
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon pepper
  • Lumpia Wrappers

Steps

  1. Put your ground meat in a bowl
  2. Combine carrots, onions, salt, pepper, and eggs in the bowl of ground meat
  3. Mix ingredients until t5horoughly combined
  4. Place one lumpia wrapper on a plate
  5. Add 2 table spoon of combined ingredients at the bottom of the lumpia wrapper
  6. Fold like a burrito
  7. Fry in 175 degree oil bath
  8. Plate and serve
