Description

Sometimes plain ramen isn't just enough. Here is a recipie to add some spices in your ramen.

Ingredients

  1. Ramen package of your choosing
  2. 1 softboiled egg
  3. Salt
  4. Green onions
  5. Beansprouts

Steps

  • Cook the ramen per the instructions
  • Once the ramen is cooked to al dente, add in a pinch of salt
  • Return pot into a simmer. Add in chopped green onions and beansprouts.
  • Serve on in bowl and add the soft boiled egg on top
